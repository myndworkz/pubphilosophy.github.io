Learning Plans Tailored to You

I offer a range of courses designed for both individual and group learning, providing structured and engaging experiences tailored to every level. Whether you’re aiming to improve your English or delve into the depths of philosophy, I have a course that’s right for you:

English Language Courses

Designed for all levels—Beginner (A1, A2), Intermediate (B1, B2), and Advanced (C1, C2). Each course builds your skills in speaking, listening, reading, and writing, tailored to your current level and language goals. Available for 1-to-1 or group sessions.

Philosophy Courses

Explore timeless questions and big ideas with 6-week courses, meeting once a week for 2-hour sessions. Choose from topics such as Modern Philosophy, Ancient Philosophy, Metaphysics, and Philosophy of Mind.

All lessons are customized to suit your preferred learning style, ability level, and personal interests. I understand the importance of flexibility, so term schedules are adaptable to your needs.
