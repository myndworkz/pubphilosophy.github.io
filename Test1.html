What is Pub Philosophy?

Pub Philosophy was founded in 1997 by Dr. Bryn Williams, a postgraduate student at King’s College London. Starting at the Glassblower in Carnaby Street, Pub Philosophy created a unique space for lively discussions on life’s toughest and strangest questions, attracting a passionate community of thinkers.

After decades of inspiring conversations in London, Pub Philosophy has now expanded to Geneva. With events held in both cities, we invite you to explore ideas, challenge your assumptions, and connect with curious minds over a drink.
