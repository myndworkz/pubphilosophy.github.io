About

Bryn Williams is credited (along with Rick Lewis , editor of Philosophy Now magazine) with first bringing Pub Philosophy to London in the late '90s. He has achieved the trifecta of Philosophy qualifications: BA, MPhil and PhD, the latter two from King's College London. For the last 17 years he has worked as a data analyst at a large London Acute NHS trust.

Hoxton Cabin is a bar, café & events space located in the heart of East London. If you’re looking for a night of fun and entertainment, Hoxton Cabin offers it all — a great vibe, live music, stand-up comedy, philosophy nights, exhibitions, screenings and a (short but sweet) food & beverages menu. https://www.hoxtoncabin.com/about

First Love Foundation is a Tower Hamlets based charity. They have spent the past 10 years helping thousands of people facing crisis to get their lives back on track. Any profit from the Philosophy Cabin will go to help them continue their work. https://www.firstlovefoundation.org.uk/ourmodel#
